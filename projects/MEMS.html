MEMS-Based 2-Axis Tilt Mechanism with Integrated Piezoresistive Sensing

A microelectromechanical (MEMS) system designed for precise two-axis angular positioning and measurement. The system utilizes electrostatic comb-drive actuators and integrated piezoresistive sensing to provide accurate tilt control and real-time position feedback for microsystem applications.

Key Features

  • Precise two-axis tilt motion control
  • Integrated piezoresistive sensing for angle feedback
  • Electrostatic vertical comb-drive actuation
  • Compact and low-power MEMS design
  • Suitable for closed-loop positioning applications

Design & Engineering

  • Mirror platform supported by torsion beams
  • Eight electrostatic comb-drive actuators
  • Tilt actuation about both X and Y axes
  • Integrated sensing through strain-induced resistance variation
  • MEMS fabrication process developed for device realization

Applications

  • Optical beam steering and LiDAR systems
  • Biomedical imaging and micro-endoscopy
  • Laser scanning systems
  • Free-space optical communication
  • Precision microsystem positioning platforms
