Oil Automation System

Project Overview

The Oil Automation System is an industrial automation solution used to manage and monitor oil distribution for equipment maintenance operations at Colombo International Container Terminals (CICT). The system combines PLC control, load-cell measurement, barcode identification, HMI interfaces, and automated dispensing mechanisms to ensure accurate, secure, and traceable oil distribution while minimizing manual intervention.

Problem Statement

Large-scale container terminal operations require frequent lubrication and oil distribution for maintenance activities. Traditional manual issuing methods can lead to inaccurate measurements, poor record keeping, delays, and potential misuse of resources. The objective of this project was to develop an automated system capable of accurately dispensing oil while maintaining complete tracking and accountability throughout the process.

System Workflow

  • Maintenance personnel submit an oil request.
  • Warehouse issues a barcode containing transaction information.
  • Barcode is scanned at the dispensing station.
  • PLC verifies the request and authorizes dispensing.
  • Load cells continuously measure the dispensed quantity.
  • Oil distribution is automatically controlled and monitored.
  • Transaction data is recorded for future reference and auditing.

System Components

  • Delta PLC for process control and automation.
  • Load cell system for precise quantity measurement.
  • Human Machine Interface (HMI) for monitoring and operation.
  • Barcode scanner for identification and authorization.
  • Thermal printer for barcode generation and transaction records.
  • Automated dispensing and monitoring hardware.

Key Features

  • Automated oil dispensing process.
  • Accurate quantity measurement using load cells.
  • Barcode-based tracking and authorization.
  • Real-time monitoring through HMI.
  • Digital record keeping and data storage.
  • Improved accountability and resource management.
  • Enhanced reliability and operational efficiency.

Technologies Used

Engineering Challenges

  • Maintaining high dispensing accuracy under varying operating conditions.
  • Ensuring reliable communication between system components.
  • Achieving robust barcode reading and transaction verification.
  • Providing secure and traceable oil distribution records.
  • Designing a user-friendly interface for maintenance personnel.

Results & Impact

The Oil Automation System significantly improves maintenance operations by providing accurate and automated oil dispensing with complete transaction tracking. The solution enhances operational efficiency, reduces manual errors, improves inventory accountability, and supports better resource management within terminal maintenance activities.
